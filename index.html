Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

HIITTrack is a no-frills HIIT timer built by one developer who got tired of bloated fitness apps. It does one thing and does it well: helps you train smarter.

  • 📊 Real-time intensity tracking Your phone's accelerometer measures effort at 50Hz. No heart rate strap, no Apple Watch — just your phone in your pocket.
  • 🏷 Exercise tagging "What did you just do?" — tap to label each round during rest. 14 built-in exercises, or add your own.
  • 🧠 AI-powered insights Were your burpees explosive enough? Did your plank wobble? Tailored feedback and rep estimates for every exercise type.
  • ✎ Fully customizable Work intervals (10–180s), rest intervals (10–180s), total time (5–60 min). Audio cues, haptic feedback, and countdown beeps keep you on pace.
  • 📋 Workout history & charts Every session saved. Intensity-by-round bars, per-exercise sparklines, mini charts on every history row.
  • 🔒 Zero data collection 100% on-device. No accounts, no cloud, no analytics SDKs. Your workouts stay on your phone.
