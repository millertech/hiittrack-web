HIITTrack is a no-frills HIIT timer built by one developer who got tired of bloated fitness apps. It does one thing and does it well: helps you train.

  • ▶ Reliable countdown timer Work and rest intervals that fire every time, no lag, no crashes.
  • ⚡ Tabata & classic HIIT presets Jump straight in with standard 20/10 Tabata or classic 40/20 circuits.
  • ✎ Custom intervals Set your own work/rest durations, number of rounds, and rest between sets.
  • 📋 Workout history & basic stats See how many sessions you've logged, total time trained, and weekly streaks.
  • ⌚ Apple Watch support Glance at your current interval right from your wrist without touching your phone.
  • 🔒 Zero data collection Your workouts stay on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics SDKs.
